What we offer

Two focused products and services for modern infrastructure and AI

Solid Distributed Systems is rebranding around two clear offers: advanced IT consultancy with tiered packages, and ADR (AI Data Refinery), a curated data product built for AI systems.
Advanced IT consultancy
Expert consulting for cloud platforms, DevOps, platform engineering, Kubernetes, automation, security, and operating model improvements. Engagements are designed as tiered packages so clients can choose the right level of advisory, implementation, and ongoing support.
ADR, AI Data Refinery
Curated and continuously updated data for AI systems, packaged for reliable downstream consumption. ADR includes MCP and API access, making it easier to connect LLM-based tools, copilots, agents, and internal AI platforms to trustworthy, maintained data sources.
Solid Distributed Systems SRL is a senior consultancy and AI data partner, bringing together experienced specialists across cloud, platform engineering, automation, data delivery, and AI integration.
